I am an assistant professor in the Department of Medicine at the University of Wisconsin, Madison. My clinical focus is in medical oncology, and my lab is focused on the development of novel liquid biopsy biomarkers for PI3K pathway targeted therapies in breast and prostate cancer.

I completed my Medical Oncology fellowship and post-doctoral research at the University of Wisconsin. Before coming to Madison, I completed my MD/PhD at the University of Chicago MSTP program. I did my graduate work in the Committee on Cancer Biology studying the role of autophagy in metastasis.
